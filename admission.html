Admission Process

Our school follows a simple and transparent admission process. Parents can apply for admission by filling the admission form available at the school office or on the school website.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nursery: 3+ years
  • LKG: 4+ years
  • UKG: 5+ years
  • Class I onwards: As per previous class passed

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Report Card
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card (Student & Parent)
  • Passport Size Photographs

Important Points

  • Admission is based on seat availability.
  • Original documents must be produced at the time of admission.
  • Admission once granted cannot be transferred.
